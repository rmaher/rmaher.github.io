About

Hello wrestling fans and welcome to the official inaugural launch of a new pro wrestling portal known as DieHard Pro Wrestling!
Brought to you by the former director and booker of NWA Cyberspace, DieHard Pro Wrestling intends on bringing new and innovative products designed specifically for the “die-hard” fans. Featured matches, live events, new DVD titles, shoot interviews and exclusive content are just some of the projects currently gearing up on the DieHard production slate.
